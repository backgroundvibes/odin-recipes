Chicken Tikka

Description

Chicken Tikka Kebab is a delicious appetizer that is packed with flavor. It starts with chicken pieces marinated in yogurt along with lemon juice and aromatic spices, then threaded onto skewers and cooked to create a simple meal that'll leave you wanting more. Make it in the air fryer or oven to serve at the gathering, or include it into your meal prep for the week.

  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
